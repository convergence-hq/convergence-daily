What would falsify it

NVIDIA, Lumentum, or Coherent publicly reduces or restructures the March 2, 2026 $4B commitment in a newsroom release, 8-K, 10-Q, or earnings-call disclosure — this is the single biggest load-bearing support for the photonic revaluation. LITE or COHR misses guidance on hyperscaler optical revenue or OCS backlog conversion by more than 25% in any subsequent quarter, eliminating the revenue-inflection case. A non-photonic interconnect alternative (advanced copper, in-package electrical fabric, or hyperscaler-internal optical engines) wins material design-in slots away from LITE or COHR at OFC 2027 or an equivalent industry disclosure event. Anduril postpones an IPO or primary financing event past end-2026 with no replacement private valuation mark, breaking the defense-AI private-mark catalyst clock the public adjacents revalue against. Hyperscaler nuclear PPA flow falls below 1 GW of newly announced contracted or development-stage capacity for two consecutive quarters, removing demand-visibility anchor for OKLO and SMR.
